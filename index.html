Game Rules

A random number is generated for each game

There are 4 crystals that are each worth a certain amount

Whenever you click each crystal it adds the amount its worth to your number

Your goal is to match the randomly generated number with your number by clicking the crystals

You win by matching the random number and lose by going over the random number.
